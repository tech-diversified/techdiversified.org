About the Program

Tech Diversified is a Seattle-based nonprofit program aimed at improving representation in the tech industry for people of color. We provide adults (18+) with free resources such as technical workshops, non-technical workshops, career counseling, one-on-one mentoring and networking meet-ups. Our goal is to empower people just starting out with the necessary tools to make informed decisions about seeking future careers in the technology field, and to help get them on the right track. We are also an ecosystem for "experienced" tech professionals to network and become mentors.
