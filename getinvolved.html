Tech Diversified is looking for adults (18+) interested in learning about topics such as computer programming, user interface design, computer hardware or data analysis to participate in our free technical workshops. These 2 to 3 night classes will cover a variety of topics, allowing participants to sample potential areas of interest.

We also offer non-technical workshops to help participants get an understanding of business culture, how to start a business, ‘learn the lingo’, or learn about subjects such as team communication.

Visit our events page or the meetup site to find out about upcoming technical and non-technical workshops.

If you would like to attend workshops, participate in our open lab time, connect with a mentor, or consult with a career counselor, we’d like you to become a member of Tech Diversified. To become a member, simply fill out the quick sign-up form using the button below, and we’ll be in contact with you.
