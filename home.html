PROMOTING DIVERSITY IN TECH

Tech Diversified connects people of color to software development through tuition-free classes, networking and professional development

  • Get Involved
More

Tech Diversified is a non-profit organization created by five friends who were frustrated with the lack of racial diversity in their workplaces, and wanted a way to address this problem while contributing back to the community.

Our solution is to build a network of diverse role models, raise technical literacy, and prepare people of color to take advantage of the many opportunities within the tech sector.

Tech Diversified helps adults (18+) plan for a career in the tech industry and supports their personal and professional development through one-on-one mentoring, technical and business workshops, and career counseling. A team of volunteers skilled in computer programming, user experience & design, and mobile application development are available to answer questions and provide guidance for program participants.

Diversity figures by ethnicity
In 2014, some of the world's leading technology companies released their diversity metrics, highlighting the fact that people of color and women make up just a small fraction of the tech workforce.
Additionally, according to the 2010 Census report, African American and Hispanic populations have among the lowest median income levels and the highest unemployment rates in the United States.
Tech Diversified wants to help address these community issues one person at a time by helping to cultivate skilled technologists and then funneling them into well-paying tech positions.

Although our target audience is people of color, our primary goal is to have a positive learning environment that welcomes everyone regardless of race, age, religion, sexual orientation, disability or education.
