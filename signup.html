Join Us

Tech Diversified is looking for adults interested in learning more about programming, user interface design, computer hardware or data analysis to participate in our free workshops. Our 2 - 3 night classes will cover a variety of topics, allowing participants to sample potential areas of interest. Visit our events page or the meetup site to find out about upcoming workshops.

We want to connect with people of all technical skill levels. For those just starting out, our introductory workshops will help give an overview of the programming or design world. See which topics interest you, and then have an opportunity to speak with professionals in those fields!

Intermediate or experienced participants will benefit from having one-on-one mentoring, engaging in mock interviews, receiving professional career counseling, and suggesting advanced workshop topics.

Established professionals are also encouraged to join our meetup group to network with other professionals or mentor up-and-coming coders. We also are always on the lookout for people to teach both technical and non-technical workshops. Please contact us to find out more information.
